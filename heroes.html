Our Heroes

The elite cats that travel to space are the most highly trained and decorated space explorers in the world...no...the universe! Below is just a brief glimpse into the brave heroes that make up Space Force.

Commander Meowingtons

Leader of Space Cats and the most decorated space explorer of all time. Meowingtons has successfully lead missions to the Moon, Mars, and a giant ball of string.

Lieutenant Whiskers

Second in command of Space Force. Whiskers is always capable and has saved his crew from certain disaster on countless occassions. He was the first cat on the moon.

Maverick

Pilot for Space Force and all around daredevil. Maverick can pilot any aircraft and is unfazed by the dangers of space.
